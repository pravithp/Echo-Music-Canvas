How to add a Canvas

01

Open the Portal

Head to the Contribute Portal and log in with your GitHub account. The portal handles forking, branching, and Pull Requests for you automatically.

02

Pick Your Canvas

Upload a new video (.mp4 or .m3u8, max 5MB, 9:16 vertical) — or reuse an existing canvas from the gallery by searching for it. No duplicate uploads needed.

03

Link Multiple Songs

Add one or many songs that should use this canvas. Perfect for album canvases — just add every track in one go, and they all share the same visual.

04

Enable in App

Once merged, open Echo Music → Settings → Playback Settings → toggle Enable Canvas. Your visualizer goes live instantly.
